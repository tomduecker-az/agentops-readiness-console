# Current Workflow Steps

1. Sales marks the customer as closed-won and submits the onboarding handoff form.
2. Customer operations reviews account details, contract notes, and implementation scope.
3. The onboarding coordinator checks whether required intake fields are complete.
4. Missing or unclear intake information is routed back to the account executive.
5. The implementation specialist reviews requested integrations and technical requirements.
6. Customers with sensitive data, custom integrations, or enterprise terms are routed for supervisor review.
7. The supervisor approves, rejects, or requests clarification on the onboarding plan.
8. The onboarding coordinator schedules the kickoff meeting and assigns internal owners.
9. The implementation specialist drafts the onboarding task plan.
10. Customer success reviews customer-facing commitments before they are sent externally.
11. The onboarding plan is shared with the customer.
12. Weekly onboarding status is tracked until launch.
