# SaaS Customer Onboarding Process Narrative

The customer onboarding process begins after a new SaaS customer signs an agreement and is handed off from sales to customer operations. The onboarding team reviews the customer profile, confirms implementation scope, identifies integration requirements, schedules kickoff activities, and assigns internal owners.

The process involves multiple handoffs between sales, customer success, implementation specialists, support, and technical teams. Some customers require elevated review due to enterprise contract terms, sensitive data requirements, custom integrations, or accelerated launch timelines.

AI assistance may help summarize account context, identify missing intake information, suggest onboarding tasks, flag risks, and draft implementation plans. However, approval gates are required before customer-facing commitments are made, before implementation scope is changed, or before sensitive customer data is shared across systems.
