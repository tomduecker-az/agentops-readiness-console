# Policy and Controls

Customer onboarding workflows may include confidential commercial information, personally identifiable information, and sensitive technical integration details.

Customer-facing commitments must be reviewed by customer success before being sent externally.

Customers with sensitive data requirements, enterprise contract terms, custom integrations, or accelerated launch timelines require supervisor approval before the onboarding plan is finalized.

AI-generated onboarding plans may be used as drafts only. A human reviewer must approve task assignments, timeline commitments, customer-facing language, and any changes to implementation scope.

Sensitive customer identifiers, contact information, integration credentials, and security details must not be sent to external AI tools unless explicitly approved and redacted according to policy.

Write actions require explicit human approval. Audit events must capture approval, policy checks, and any project-management or customer-facing write action.
