# Current Workflow Steps

1. Analyst receives or identifies an unmatched payment record.
2. Analyst checks the source system for matching transaction details.
3. Analyst compares the record against an off-system tracking spreadsheet.
4. Analyst determines the exception type.
5. Analyst updates status and notes.
6. Items above the approval threshold are routed to a supervisor.
7. Supervisor reviews and approves or rejects the proposed resolution.
8. Analyst completes the resolution update.
9. Weekly exception report is prepared.
